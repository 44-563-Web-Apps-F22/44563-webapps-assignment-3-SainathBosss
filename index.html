Titanic Museum

The exhibition is well laid out and covers six floors over nine interactive galleries. The exhibition uses special effects, dark rides, full-scale reconstructions and interactive features. Visitors explore the shipyard, dive to the sea bed and uncover the true story, and legends, of the Titanic in the city where the ship was “born and raised”. This Titanic Museum is famous for its authenticity.
